New Development - 2025

New AIRLESS MINI BALLS

Starting 2024, Bola Gema started producing AIRLESS Mini Balls. The PU CORE Production setup along with other modules of Airless Mini Balls production were set up and production started in 2024.

New Development - 2022

In-house production of TPU Laminated COVER material

We are pleased to announce the in-house production of TPU Laminated COVER material for Sports Balls and Sports Bags. The LAMINATION PLANT helps produce 4000 meters (25000 balls) material per day. We also offer TPU Laminated Material to the sports Balls and Bags industry in Pakistan, China, India, Vietnam, Thailand, Japan, and Malaysia.

New Development - 2019

FIFA LICENSE PROGRAM

Our business is based on the principle of offering the highest quality products. In 2019, we joined the FIFA QUALITY PROGRAM to ensure the highest quality standards. Bola Gema is now a FIFA Licensee for FIFA QUALITY PRO soccer balls, FIFA IMS soccer balls, and FIFA IMS Soccer Balls (SMART SEAM STITCHED).

FIFA BALL TESTING LABORATORY

A fully functional SOCCER BALL TESTING Laboratory was established in-house to perform FIFA QUALITY TESTS. This laboratory is used for our own needs and provides FIFA testing services to clients seeking FIFA certification for their products.

RUBBER PRODUCTION LINE

A fully renovated and compliant RUBBER PRODUCTION LINE became operational in February 2020. It produces various types of synthetic rubber bladders for soccer balls, handballs, volleyballs, basketballs, and more. We also produce rubberized basketballs and playground balls.

ENVIRONMENT FRIENDLY PRODUCTION

By the end of 2022, Bola Gema plans to convert production to SOLAR ENERGY, aiming to reduce environmental impact and production costs.
